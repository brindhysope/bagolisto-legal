# Privacy Policy for Bagolisto

Effective date: May 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Bagolisto mobile application handles data when it is used.

Bagolisto is published by Brindhysope.

Contact: devbrindy@gmail.com

## 1. Summary

Bagolisto is an Android application for packing lists and travel preparation.

By default:

- trips, bags, items, notes, colors, and preferences are stored locally on the device;
- Bagolisto does not require a user account;
- Bagolisto does not sell personal data;
- Bagolisto does not display advertising;
- diagnostics and usage analytics are disabled by default;
- users can enable or disable diagnostics and usage analytics from the app settings.

## 2. Data created by the user

The user may create the following data in the app:

- trips;
- bags;
- items to pack;
- quantities;
- notes;
- colors or visual classification information;
- display and export preferences.

This data is stored locally on the device. It is not sent to Brindhysope by default.

Bagolisto does not require the user to enter their name, address, email address, or account identifier to use the local features of the app.

## 3. Diagnostics data

Bagolisto may use Firebase Crashlytics to receive crash and stability reports.

Diagnostics are disabled by default. They are enabled only if the user activates the corresponding option in the app settings.

When enabled, diagnostics may include:

- technical information about a crash;
- device type;
- Android system version;
- app version;
- technical logs required to analyze the issue.

Diagnostics reports are not intended to contain the content of the user's trips, bags, lists, or notes.

When the user disables diagnostics, Bagolisto disables Crashlytics report sending and requests deletion of unsent reports when technically available.

## 4. Usage analytics

Bagolisto may use Firebase Analytics to understand general app usage.

Usage analytics are disabled by default. They are enabled only if the user activates the corresponding option in the app settings.

When enabled, analytics may include non-sensitive events, for example:

- app opening;
- screen viewing;
- use of a general feature;
- technical information about the app version or device type.

Usage analytics must not include the content of the user's trips, bags, lists, items, or notes.

Bagolisto disables collection of the Android Advertising ID when applicable to the app configuration.

## 5. In-app purchases

Bagolisto may offer in-app purchases or subscriptions through Google Play Billing.

Payment operations are handled by Google Play. Bagolisto does not receive or store full payment card details.

Bagolisto may store locally or technically process the Pro access state required for the app to work, for example to determine whether certain features are available.

## 6. Data sharing

Bagolisto does not sell personal data.

Some technical data may be processed by service providers required for the operation of the app, including:

- Google Firebase, for diagnostics and usage analytics when enabled by the user;
- Google Play, for in-app purchases and subscriptions.

These providers process data according to their own terms and privacy policies.

## 7. Data retention

Local data created in Bagolisto remains on the device as long as the user keeps the app and its data.

The user can delete data by deleting the relevant items in the app, when the feature is available, or by deleting the app data from Android settings.

Technical data processed by Firebase or Google Play is retained according to the settings and policies of those services.

## 8. Security

Bagolisto limits data collection to what is necessary to provide its features.

The main travel data is stored locally on the device. The user remains responsible for the general security of their device, including screen lock, Android backups, and physical access to the phone.

No method of storage or transmission can be guaranteed to be fully secure.

## 9. Children

Bagolisto is not specifically intended for children.

If a future version of Bagolisto targets children or families, this policy will be updated and the corresponding Google Play declarations will be adjusted.

## 10. User rights

Depending on applicable law, users may have rights regarding their data, including the right to access, rectify, delete, or object to processing.

For any privacy-related request, users may contact:

devbrindy@gmail.com

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect changes to the app, the services used, or legal requirements.

The effective date shown at the top of this page will be updated in case of significant changes.
