Bagolisto Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-07-09 · Version 1.0

This policy explains how the Android application Bagolisto processes data. Bagolisto is published by Brindhysope.

1. Essential principles

Bagolisto mainly works locally, does not require any Bagolisto account, does not post any advertising and does not sell any personal data.

2. Data stored locally

Travel, destinations, luggage, items, tasks, notes, quantities, colors and preferences are recorded locally on the aircraft. Android widgets display locally the information chosen by the user.

3. Stability diagnostics

Firebase Crashlytics receives by default technical reports to detect crashes, correct errors, improve stability and identify compatibility problems. This processing is not a user setting. Bagolisto does not define any custom Crashlytics user ID and does not send email address or Google account.

4. Use statistics

Firebase Analytics deals only with generic and non-sensitive functional events. Statistics are disabled by default everywhere. The user can enable or disable them at any time in Settings. They are never used for advertising, remarketing, advertising hearings or customization.

5. Content never collected

Diagnostics and statistics never receive travel titles, personal destinations, names of items, baggage or tasks, notes, entered texts, PDF content, backup content, imported or exported data, or Google Drive account. Bagolisto does not collect Advertising ID.

6. Google Play purchases

Google Play processes Bagolisto Plus purchases and subscriptions. Bagolisto does not receive or retain complete credit card data. The application deals only with the access status required for its features.

7. Google Drive backup

The backup is optional and reserved for Bagolisto Plus when offered. At the request of the user, Google Drive keeps a copy of the travels and library. It is not an automatic synchronisation between devices. Backup content is never used for diagnostics or statistics.

8. Export PDF and Android widgets

PDFs are generated locally and then shared only at the user's initiative. Their content is not sent to Brindhysope, Crashlytics or Analytics. Android widgets read and display local data.

9. Technical providers

Google Firebase provides Crashlytics and Analytics, Google Play processes purchases and Google Drive hosts requested backups. These providers process their data according to their own policies. Bagolisto is linked to no Google Ads, AdMob, SDK advertising or advertising audience services.

10. Conservation, security and suppression

Local data remain on the device until deleted in the application, erasing Android data or uninstalling. The backups can be deleted from the service concerned. The technical data shall follow the shelf-life of the providers. No mode of storage or transmission can guarantee absolute security.

11. User rights

Depending on the applicable law, the user may request access, rectification, erasure, limitation or opposition to the data held by the publisher. The editor cannot remotely delete local data that he does not hold.

12. Contact and changes

For any questions or requests regarding data, contact devbrindy@gmail.com. This policy may evolve to reflect application, providers or legal obligations; its effective date will then be updated.

In the event of a translation gap, the French version prevails to the extent permitted by law.
